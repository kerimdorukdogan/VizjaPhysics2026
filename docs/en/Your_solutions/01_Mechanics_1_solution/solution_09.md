# ⚖️ Problem 9 — Momentum Comparison

We compare the momentum of two objects:

• a **2-gram fly** flying at **10 m/s**  
• a **60-gram tennis ball** moving at **1 m/s**

The momentum formula is

$$
p = mv
$$

---

## 1️⃣ Convert masses to kilograms

Fly:

$$
2 g = 0.002 kg
$$

Tennis ball:

$$
60 g = 0.060 kg
$$

---

## 2️⃣ Compute the fly momentum

$$
p_{fly} = mv
$$

$$
p_{fly} = 0.002 \times 10
$$

$$
p_{fly} = 0.020
$$

---

## 3️⃣ Compute the tennis ball momentum

$$
p_{ball} = mv
$$

$$
p_{ball} = 0.060 \times 1
$$

$$
p_{ball} = 0.060
$$

---

## 4️⃣ Compare the values

$$
0.060 > 0.020
$$

So the tennis ball has greater momentum.

To see how much greater:

$$
\frac{0.060}{0.020} = 3
$$

---

## 🎯 Final Result

| Object | Momentum |
|------|------|
| Fly | 0.020 kg·m/s |
| Tennis ball | 0.060 kg·m/s |

The **tennis ball has three times greater momentum**.
