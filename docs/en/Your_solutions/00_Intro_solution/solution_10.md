# 🐜 Problem 10 — Infinite Series

The ant moves:

- 1 m east
- 1/2 m north
- 1/3 m west
- 1/4 m south
- ...

---

## 🔍 Step-by-Step Solution

Horizontal movement:

$$
1 - \frac{1}{3} + \frac{1}{5} - \frac{1}{7} + ...
$$

Vertical movement:

$$
\frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{6} - \frac{1}{8} + ...
$$

These are alternating harmonic-type series.

---

## 🎯 Final Result

The motion converges to a finite position due to convergence of the alternating series.
