# ⏱ Problem 4 — Rearranging Formulas

The period of a simple pendulum is:

$$
T = 2\pi \sqrt{\frac{l}{g}}
$$

We want to rearrange the formula to express **g**.

---

## 🔍 Step-by-Step Solution

Divide both sides by \(2\pi\):

$$
\frac{T}{2\pi} = \sqrt{\frac{l}{g}}
$$

Square both sides:

$$
\left(\frac{T}{2\pi}\right)^2 = \frac{l}{g}
$$

Solve for \(g\):

$$
g = \frac{l}{(T/2\pi)^2}
$$

Simplify:

$$
g = \frac{4\pi^2 l}{T^2}
$$

---

## 🎯 Final Result

$$
g = \frac{4\pi^2 l}{T^2}
$$
